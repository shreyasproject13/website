About Suryoday Enterprise

Our Story

In every day life everyone is busy with their schedule. No one has time for proper cooking and eating. So we shift towards fast food and it is regular practice now. There is no option for this. So we thought that we can provide good healthy breakfast for our family members by providing delicious and healthy products.

2020

Our Beginning

We started firstly from fresh millet flours in January 2020. We began preparing different types of flours from wheat, Jowar, Nachani, Bajari, Rice, lentiles, and Suntwada.

2021

Expanding to Turmeric

From 2021 we started to make turmeric powder from turmeric sticks. We bring good quality turmeric sticks directly from farmers and produce non-mixed, high-quality turmeric powder with no adulterants or preservatives. We supplied this turmeric powder to various happy customers of Suryoday enterprises.

2022

Packaging and Sevai Production

In 2022, we started packaging turmeric powder and supplying it to local area shops within appropriate time. Also, a new machine was added for manufacturing of sevai. From this, we started our own production of sevai.

2023

New Machinery and Products

In 2023, we added a few machines to our group: a papad rolling machine and a dal making machine. We started selling papad and dal produced from these machines.

2024

Retail Expansion

In 2024, we started packing Shevai in small pouches and distributing them to local area grocery stores. Along with this, we began selling different types of papad through local advertisements and grocery shops.

2025

Product Innovation

From this year, we decided to give a variety of sevai to our valuable customers which are free of preservatives and made of food items such as vegetables, fruits, flavors, and powders.

Our Promise

We are committed to providing healthy, preservative-free products to our customers. Our mission is to ensure that even in today's busy world, families can enjoy nutritious and delicious food options.
